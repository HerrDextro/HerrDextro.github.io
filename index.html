According to my research most use tools like excel and can spend hours a month just making quotas. So I decided I would try automate it with AI and make it even so easy that a quote can be created and sent still on the jobsite with only a phone and one hand free. The result of that is Quotli.ch, which turns natural language into a formatted quota ready to send.
